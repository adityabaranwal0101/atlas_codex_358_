Welcome to Atlas Codex

At Atlas Codex 358 we believe in inspiring curiosity, building a community of space enthusiasts, and advocating for continued exploration while showcasing the practical applications and technological innovations stemming from space research. Our journey has begun in 2023 with a vision to inform and engage visitors by sharing the latest updates, discoveries, and educational content related to the cosmos. The site offers resources for students, educators, and those interested in learning more about space, fostering collaboration and supporting the significance of space exploration for both scientific progress and humanity's future.

Our Values

At Atlas Codex, our values drive everything we do. We stand for integrity, user satisfaction, transparency and honesty etc and these principles guide our decisions and relationships with our users, partners, and team members.
